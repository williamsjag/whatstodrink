Welcome to What's to drink?!

Your account comes equipped with a selection of 41 classic cocktails and 140 common ingredients.

To set which ingredients you have in stock, click on "Manage Ingredients" in the navigation bar.

To add a new ingredient of your own, use "Add Ingredient", and to add a cocktail recipe, "Add Cocktail."

Most importantly, clicking the "What's to drink?" logo at the top of the screen will show you all the cocktails in your database that you can make with what you have on hand. For inspiration on what to pick up next, "What's Missing" will show you the cocktails you could make if you just had that last missing piece.
