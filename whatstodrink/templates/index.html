Welcome to What's to drink?!

Your account comes equipped with a selection of 40 classic cocktails and 150 common ingredients.

To set which ingredients you have 'in stock,' click on "Manage Ingredients" in the navigation bar.

To add a new ingredient of your own, go to "Add Ingredient", and to add a cocktail recipe, make your way to "Add Cocktail."

Most importantly, clicking the "What's to drink?" logo at the top of the screen will show you all the cocktails in your database that you can make with what you have on hand.
